ABOUT US

MISSION STATEMENT

”CADC is a competitive dance team composed solely of UC Irvine undergraduate students. We empower our dancers of different backgrounds by providing a space to cultivate their potential as leaders who recognize both the importance of tradition and the necessity for change. We strive to uphold the values of family, history, and growth as students of dance.“
